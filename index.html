About Abhiyanthriki

Abhiyanthriki, the annual technical festival of Rajagiri School of Engineering & Technology (RSET), is a premier platform celebrating academic brilliance, cutting-edge technology, and creative innovation. Spanning two dynamic days, it showcases vibrant workshops, technical and non-technical contests, exhibitions, and stalls, drawing participation from across the region.
